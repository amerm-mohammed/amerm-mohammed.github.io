Trip to Istanbul - Turkey

Istanbul is one of the largest and most beautiful cities in Turkey and is ranked among the most attractive tourist cities in the world, and Istanbul is the first choice for tourists who are thinking of going to Turkey.

Our tour will be in the following sights:

Hagia Sophia Museum

Known in the Ottoman era as the Great Mosque of the Noble Hagia Sophia, it is located on the European bank in Istanbul and is one of the most famous landmarks in Turkey that everyone is keen to visit during tourism tours in Istanbul

Topkapi Palace

One of the best tourist places in Istanbul is recommended to visit to find out how the affairs of life and governance were managed under the Ottoman Caliphate in Turkey for 4 centuries. The great museum reviews its events and details.

Galata Tower

The Galata Tower is an ancient historical tower said to be built in 507 AD, then the tower was subjected to a strong earthquake that greatly affected it, after which it was restored and built in the Romanesque style in the 14th century, and at this time it was the tallest building in Istanbul.

Eyup Sultan Mosque

An old Ottoman mosque is located in the Eyup region on the European side of Istanbul, near the Golden Horn, and is located outside the walls of Constantinople, as the mosque was built in 1458, that is, five years after the conquest of Constantinople, and it is the first mosque built by Muslims in Istanbul after the conquest of Constantinople The year 1453.
